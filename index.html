How it works

  1. Sign in Your session stays on this device so you can pick up where you left off.
  2. Link & dispose Choose a bin, drop recyclables or waste as directed, and finish the live session.
  3. Earn & redeem Points add to your balance; redeem them for rewards and view codes under My Rewards.

You can also submit trash with photo proof for admin review.
